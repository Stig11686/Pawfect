PAWFECT PET CARE!

Skiptons Number 1 Pet Care Service

Fully Licensed and insured, I offer a free home consultation with your pet so your pet can be fully comfortable before our first outing.

I have been an avid dog lover for over 20 years, have a young pup named Brady and support various dog charities and events in the area.

Our Offer

Dog Walking

I believe in researching and applying different walking routes for your dogs, to enhance their senses and relieve boredom.

1 dog for 1 hour is just £10. Every additional dog from the same household is £5 per hour per dog.

Home Visits

Daily pet visits are a welcome service for pet owners who travel a lot, work long hours or are perhaps taking a well earned holiday or having a weekend away. My visit includes providing fresh food and water, cleaning of litter trays and garden clean up’s. I am happy to water house plants, open and close curtains/blinds and turn lights on and off should you wish me to. My visits are for a minimum of 15 minutes and can be provided once, twice or three times a day. I will also administer medication to your pet. A daily update can be provided to you to ensure peace of mind while you are away. Home visits from £10.

Doggy Day Care

If you are coming to Skipton for lunch or some retail therapy and would like to bring your dog along I would be happy to take him/her for a walk while you are having some “me time”. My location is in central Skipton and therefore I have some wonderful walks on my doorstep such as Skipton Woods for your dog to explore and enjoy while you are relaxing. Charges from £11 per dog per day. Drop off from 7.30am, pick up between 4pm and 6pm.

Dog Boarding

I am happy to have your pets overnight at my home. This service gets booked up very quickly as I am very limited in space! This includes a dog walk, and overnight stays are charged at £14 per hour.
